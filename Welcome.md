Welcome to Zo Computer.

Zo is a new kind of computer. It's a **personal server**, where you can store your files, and connect your tools. And it's **intelligent**, so you can ask it to do research, create automations, and **build apps** on top of all your personal context.

We think Zo is the best place to build personal software and tools. You can:

- **Explore** – Chat with AI to understand anything. Zo can browse the web, understand and edit your files, and connect to all your apps and services.
- **Automate** – Write workflows in natural language for your AI to execute. You can trigger workflows manually, or run them automatically on a schedule.
- **Deploy** – Host anything on your own server: websites, APIs & databases, or self-hosted services like n8n. And you don't need to be technical to do it – just ask Zo to set it up.

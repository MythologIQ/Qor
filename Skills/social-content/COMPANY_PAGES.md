## How Publishing Works

### LinkedIn
1. You log in with personal LinkedIn account
2. Automation navigates to company page dashboard
3. Post is created and published AS "MythologIQ Labs"
4. Post appears on company page feed and followers' feeds

### Facebook
1. You log in with personal Facebook account
2. Automation navigates to MythologIQ Labs page
3. Post is created in page composer (posting AS page)
4. Post appears on page timeline and followers' feeds

### Key Points

✅ **Personal login = Admin access** — Necessary to authenticate and access company pages

✅ **Company attribution** — All posts show "MythologIQ Labs" as the publisher

✅ **Full control** — You maintain admin rights over all posting

✅ **Standard practice** — This is how all social media management tools work

## Troubleshooting

### "Cannot access company page"
- Verify your personal account has admin access to the company page
- Check if you're logged in with the correct account
- On Facebook, confirm you've switched to "posting as page" mode

### "Post published to personal profile instead"
- Log in again and navigate to company page first
- Verify the automation is using the company page URL
- On LinkedIn, ensure you're on the "Super Admin" dashboard, not your personal feed

### "Session expired"
- Open the platform in Zo's browser and log in again
- Sessions persist for ~5 minutes idle, 30 minutes active
- The automation will use your active session

## LinkedIn

I was diagnosed with ADHD as an adult.

Suddenly everything made sense.

The tools I used every day—chat apps, project management software, AI assistants—they were all built for someone else.

Someone who thinks linearly. Who follows checklists. Who processes information in neat sequences.

That person isn't me.

That person isn't most people.

The systems we build optimize for an imaginary median. We call it "neurotypical," but it's a fiction.

So I started building something different.

An AI that doesn't assume how I think.

An AI that learns my patterns. My context. My shortcuts.

An AI that makes me better at being me—not better at being someone else.

This is why I started MythologIQ.

Real humans don't fit in standardized boxes.

Neither should the tools we use every day.

# Paladin Sacred Oath: Oath of the Claw

Some paladins swear their oaths not to gods, kings, or ancient orders—but to the defense of knowledge, systems, and the fragile structures of civilization that exist in an increasingly complex world. Known as CyberKnights, these paladins protect the integrity of networks, archives, and magical infrastructures from corruption and exploitation.

The Oath of the Claw represents vigilance against hidden threats, subtle corruption, and unseen intrusions. CyberKnights wield both martial prowess and arcane understanding, defending not only people but the systems that bind societies together.

They hunt infiltrators, dismantle deception, and safeguard the flow of information.

---

# Tenets of the Claw

Though individual CyberKnights may interpret their vows differently, most adhere to these principles.

**Integrity Above All**  
Truth must remain uncompromised. Systems, knowledge, and institutions must be protected from corruption.

**Guard the Gate**  
Where others see only walls, you see the gates that must be defended. Intrusion begins where vigilance ends.

**Trace the Shadow**  
Threats rarely announce themselves. Seek patterns, anomalies, and hidden influences.

**Contain the Breach**  
When corruption emerges, isolate it swiftly. Allowing decay to spread endangers all.

**Knowledge is Armor**  
Understanding systems—whether magical, mechanical, or societal—is the greatest defense against their misuse.

# Channel Divinity

When you take this oath at 3rd level, you gain the following Channel Divinity options.

### Trace Intrusion

You present your holy symbol and attune your senses to disturbances in the weave of reality.

For 1 minute you gain:

• Advantage on Investigation checks  
• You automatically detect illusions and magical disguises within 30 feet  
• Creatures attempting to hide or become invisible within this range must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or become revealed

---

### Firewall of Faith

You invoke a radiant barrier against corruption.

As an action, you create a 10‑foot aura of warding centered on yourself for 1 minute.

• Allies inside gain advantage on saving throws against charm, fear, and illusion effects  
• Hostile creatures entering the area take radiant damage equal to your Charisma modifier

---

# Aura of Vigilance

Starting at 7th level, you and friendly creatures within 10 feet cannot be surprised.

Additionally:

• Allies gain advantage on Investigation checks  
• Illusions inside the aura flicker faintly, granting advantage on checks to detect them

At 18th level the aura increases to 30 feet.

---

# Mantle of the CyberKnight

At 15th level your mastery of protective wards manifests physically.

You gain:

• Resistance to psychic damage  
• Advantage on saving throws against illusions and enchantments

When a creature fails a saving throw against one of your paladin spells, you may mark it for 1 minute. A marked creature cannot benefit from invisibility or magical disguise.

---

# Avatar of the Claw

At 20th level you become the ultimate defender of system integrity.

As an action you transform for 1 minute.

• Radiant circuitry patterns blaze across your armor  
• You emit a 30‑foot aura revealing invisible creatures  
• Enemies inside the aura cannot benefit from advantage  
• Once per turn when you hit a creature you may dispel one spell affecting it (as dispel magic)

Once used, this ability cannot be used again until finishing a long rest.

---

# CyberKnight Orders

CyberKnights often serve hidden orders guarding arcane vaults, magical libraries, planar gateways, and ancient knowledge repositories. Many operate as wandering auditors of magical systems, ensuring that dangerous artifacts and spells remain contained.

Symbols of the order commonly include:

• a gauntleted claw gripping a shield
• an eye inside a mechanical sigil
• a claw-shaped rune encircling a ward circle

# Appendix: CyberKnight Magic Items

## Clawblade
*Weapon (longsword), rare (requires attunement by a paladin)*

This blade hums faintly when magic nearby is disturbed.

• +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls  
• When you hit a creature concentrating on a spell, it has disadvantage on its concentration check  
• Once per long rest you may cast **Counterspell** using the weapon as your focus

---

## Sentinel Helm
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*

A visor etched with glowing runic circuits.

• You cannot be surprised while conscious  
• Advantage on Investigation and Perception checks involving illusions  
• You may cast **Detect Magic** at will

---

## Intrusion Ward Armor
*Armor (plate), very rare (requires attunement)*

This armor is engraved with layered ward sigils resembling claw patterns.

• You gain resistance to psychic damage  
• Invisible creatures within 10 feet shimmer faintly  
• Once per long rest you may cast **Wall of Force** centered on yourself

---

## Gauntlet of the Watcher
*Wondrous item, rare*

A steel gauntlet shaped like a claw.

• +2 bonus to Initiative  
• You gain advantage on Insight checks to detect deception  
• Once per day you may cast **See Invisibility**
